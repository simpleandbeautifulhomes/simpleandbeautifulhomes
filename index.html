About the Designer

A Decade of Crafting Soulful Interiors

With over 10 years of dedicated practice in Indian interior design, I bring spaces to life through the rich vocabulary of India's design heritage — from the delicate geometry of Mughal jaali work to the vibrant palette of Rajasthani folk art and the serene restraint of Kerala's wooden architecture.

Every project is a collaboration. I listen deeply to understand how a family lives, what brings them joy, and how their home can be transformed into a sanctuary that is unmistakably theirs — and unmistakably Indian.

My work has been featured in Architectural Digest India, Better Homes & Gardens, and several national publications celebrating contemporary Indian design.
